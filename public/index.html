Who am I

Hi, I am Carbell Kofi Sarfo.
You can call me many things. You can call me a leader, graphics desinger, web developer, music lover or by any market defined function-title. I am multi-disciplinary maker with over 3 years of experience in wide range of design disciplines, management, entrepreneur, front-end developer, music enthusiast, photographer, you name it and you will be shocked I am there.


I don't like to define myself by the work I have done. I define myself by the work towards the goals I am headed. Skills can be taught, personality is inherent. I prefer to keep learning, continue challenging myself, and do interesting things that are both aligned and not aligned to my future goals.

Where I am at the Moment

I am currently in my final year in the University of Professional Studies pursuing a Bachelor of Art in Business Administration. My experiences here have not only thought me but built me in many areas of my life. I have been able to amass lots of soft and hard skills. Knowing about myself, building my public speaking skills, leadership skills..

Where I am headed

I see a future of IT, a future where innovation is a must, a future where business leaders have to be equipped in many disciplines and knowledge background. And that is the future I am painting.
