About Me

Who am I

Hi, I am Carbell Kofi Sarfo.
I'm Carbell Kofi Sarfo, a creative entrepreneur and content creator, web designer and developer who shares my knowledge and documents my life on my YouTube channel and on my podcast.
In addition to creating YouTube content, I serve as a Brand Strategist at Segula Global. I'm known for my versatility, taking on diverse roles including leadership, graphic design, web development, and more.


I don't like to define myself by the work I have done. I define myself by the work towards the goals I am headed. Skills can be taught, personality is inherent. I am addicted to learning, continually challenging myself, and do interesting things that are both aligned and not aligned to my future goals.

Where I am at the Moment

Currently, I serve as a Brand Strategist at Segula Global, an executive branding organization that transforms leaders into thought leaders. In this role, I have continued building both hard and soft skills, while gaining valuable leadership abilities.

Where I am headed

I see a future of IT and brands, a future where innovation is a must, a future where business leaders have to be equipped in many disciplines and knowledge background. A future where you have to have a personal brand. And that is the future I am painting.


I am on the journey of discovering the limit to my abilities. To cause a change in someones THINKING. To instill in people the believe that "you don't need to be a superhero to be able to do it". Just a simple first step and then figure it on the way.
