About

My name is Sarfo Carbell Kofi. I'm an interactive designer who currently works in Ghana.

I'm keen on designing fresh interface with an attention to detail and coherence for a brighter and appealing layout as well as designs. Some of my favourte projects include e-commerce sites and Personal Websites.


It's a year now I started this journey as a web designer and developer. I am a self taught designer who is ever ready to learn what ever it takes to make your project a memorable one.
