How it started

The journey is quite funny as a few friends were talking about "coding" and how that is used to create website. As curious as I am, I searched it up and met "Bucky Robert's html and css video for absolute beginnerrs" and was immediately hooked.
The journey then translated from being hooked to Learning to now teaching and the teaching came as a result of my unique teaching gift.
I started by teaching simple designs to give your projects an amazing feel. But I was missing something.


I wanted something that would let me be creative and push me to keep relevant, so after thinking about it for a really long time, I started my channel in October of 2016


Progress is slow
